Sanwich Burgertale


So this game is just a single boss fight on like a 35 second attack pattern loop. There was supposed to be a story mode but clearly I didn't finish it yet.
There's music so turn up the volume. I wrote and played it myself. You can hear my fingers hitting the keyboard and where the audio cuts among other issues.
(I had to edit the parts that I got right together because I never managed to play it all in one take lol.)
Anyways. There was an attempt.
Arrow Keys = Shoot (hold multiple for diagonal)
WASD = Movement
Spacebar = Special
Shoot at Sanwich until he dies. (He may or may not be mad at you for killing everyone.)
Don't reload the page if you die, instead hit the back button and then the fight button again, it breaks otherwise, don't ask me why.
The fight begins as soon as you click the button that says "fight". GLHF
